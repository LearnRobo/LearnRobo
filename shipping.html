Shipping policy

At learnrobo, we strive to offer fast and reliable shipping to our customers across India. Below you’ll find our shipping policy, including processing times, delivery options, and more.

1. Processing Time

  • All orders are processed within 1-3 business days.
  • Orders are not shipped or delivered on Sundays or public holidays.
  • If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, processing may be delayed. In such cases, we will notify you via email or phone.

2. Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates

We offer standard shipping across India. The estimated delivery times are based on your location.

  • Standard Shipping: ₹50 for orders below ₹1,000. Delivery in 4-7 business days.
  • Free Shipping: For orders over ₹1,000. Delivery in 4-7 business days.

Note: Delivery times may vary due to unforeseen factors, including weather conditions, carrier delays, or local holidays.

3. Express Shipping (Optional)

  • Express Shipping is available for ₹150, with delivery in 1-3 business days, depending on your location.

4. International Shipping

Currently, we do not offer international shipping. We only deliver within India.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a shipment confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your package using the link provided or directly through our website.

6. Delivery Issues

If there is an issue with the delivery of your order, such as lost or delayed packages, please contact our support team at support@learnrobo.in.in or call us at 9452290930. We will assist you in resolving any problems.

7. Shipping Address Changes

If you need to change your shipping address after placing an order, please contact us within 10 hours. After dispatch, we cannot guarantee address changes.

8. Order Cancellations

Orders can be canceled before they are shipped. Once shipped, you will need to follow our Return & Refund Policy.
